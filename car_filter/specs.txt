An application that lists a grid of cars from the given collection and has the capability to filter them

- Display the following for each car
  - an image
  - make and model
  - price
  - year manufactured

- The page should have a filtering area that contains the following:
  - A select box for car makers
    - options are populated by selecting all the unique makers from our
      car collection
  - A select box for car models
    - options are populated by selecting all the unique models from our
      car collection
  - A select box for years
    - options are populated by selecting all the unique years from our car
      collection
  - A select box for price
    - options are populated by selecting all the unique years from our car
      collection
  - A filter button

- When the filter button is clicked, the cars are filtered and only those cars
  are displayed that have the same price, mode, make and manufacture year as
  selected in the select boxes for the corresponding attributes.
